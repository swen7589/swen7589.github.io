My Favorite "Indie" Artists

I put indie in quotes because as time goes on, the indie genre has 
become clearly full of people who did not start from nothing. Whether their
parents are rich/famous and funded their career or they have been stars
since their childhood, the indie genre can no longer truly be considered "indie".

Anyway, here are some of those artists!

Most of these people I have been a fan of for years.

  • Finneas

  • The 1975

  • Ruel

  • Lorde

How Long I've Been A Fan

I have been a fan of the indie/alt genre for a few years now. 
I started listening to the genre through The 1975's second album back in 2016
and from there, I was hooked. I created my indie playlist in 2019 finally after
the genre fully invaded my day to day music listening. Listen to it on Spotify!
